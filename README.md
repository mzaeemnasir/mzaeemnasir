## `> whoami`

Full-stack developer and AI specialist based in the UK, building at the intersection of **voice AI**, **real-time systems**, and **algorithmic trading**. I design and ship production infrastructure — from TTS/ASR pipelines and WebSocket-driven voice agents to autonomous crypto trading engines.

I care about clean architecture, low-latency systems, and turning complex problems into reliable software.

---

## 🔭 What I'm Building

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🎙️ Voice & AI Systems
Building real-time voice AI infrastructure — TTS pipelines, ASR provider abstraction layers, VAD integration, and interactive voice agents with WebGL visualizations. Production systems handling text preprocessing, voice cloning, and streaming audio.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📊 Algorithmic Trading Systems
Building automated trading infrastructure — signal aggregation, real-time WebSocket price feeds, custom strategy engines on crypto futures, risk management with compounding models, and full-stack dashboards for live monitoring and analytics.
