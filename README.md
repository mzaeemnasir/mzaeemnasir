## `> whoami`

AI engineer and full-stack developer based in London, working at the intersection of **voice AI**, **real-time systems**, and **algorithmic trading**. I build production infrastructure: streaming TTS and ASR pipelines, WebSocket voice agents with WebGL visualisations, and automated trading engines on crypto and FX markets.

I care about clean architecture, tight latency budgets, and turning hard problems into reliable software.

`🟢 Currently:` shipping voice AI at scale by day, building trading systems by night.

---

## 🔭 What I'm Building

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🎙️ Voice & AI Systems
Real-time voice infrastructure: streaming TTS and ASR pipelines, provider abstraction layers, and voice activity detection. I work on multi-GPU model serving, speaker identification and diarisation, text preprocessing for messy real-world input, and interactive voice agents with shader-driven WebGL visualisations.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📊 Algorithmic Trading Systems
Automated trading infrastructure: custom strategy engines on crypto futures and FX, real-time WebSocket price feeds, signal aggregation, and risk management with compounding models. Plus full-stack dashboards for live monitoring, trade journalling, and analytics.

</td>
